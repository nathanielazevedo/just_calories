# Ledger - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 22, 2025**

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Ledger is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Ledger:
- Does NOT require account creation
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track your activity
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
- Does NOT share any information with third parties
- Does NOT store any data on external servers

## Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using your device's secure storage. This includes:
- Your profile information (age, gender, height, weight goals)
- Your daily calorie and exercise entries
- Your actual weight measurements

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to any cloud service by our app
- Can be completely deleted at any time using the "Clear All Data" button in Settings

## Third-Party Services

Ledger does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data. You can delete all your information at any time by:
1. Opening the app
2. Going to Settings
3. Tapping "Clear All Data"

Alternatively, deleting the app from your device will also remove all stored data.

## Children's Privacy

Ledger does not knowingly collect information from children. The app is designed for adults tracking their weight and calorie intake.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
